Whitewater Rafting Co.

Experience the thrill of whitewater rafting as you paddle through roaring rapids and breathtaking scenery. Our expert guides ensure every trip is safe, exciting, and tailored to adventurers of all skill levels. From family‑friendly floats to adrenaline‑packed runs, unforgettable river memories await—are you ready to dive in?

Whitewater Rafting Co. began in the early 1980s when a small group of river enthusiasts decided to share their love of adventure with others. With only a few rafts and a single mountain route, they built a reputation for safety, skill, and unforgettable experiences.

History

As demand grew, the company expanded into multi‑day expeditions, eco‑tours, and specialized outings for schools and corporate groups. Their commitment to environmental stewardship and community partnerships set them apart from competitors.
